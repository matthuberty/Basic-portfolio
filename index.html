Want to make a BLT? Here are the steps:
  1. Get some wheat bread out of your pantry
  2. Put two slices in your toaster and toast
  3. Get some bacon, lettuce, and tomato out of your refrigerator
  4. Microwave your bacon. I prefer to microwave because, wrapped in paper towel, cooked bacon is leaner and delicious
  5. Slice your tomatoe
  6. Grab some thin slices of lettuce
  7. By now, your toast and bacon should be done cooking
  8. Slather some mayo on your toast.
  9. Assemble that baby and eat it! Obviously, put the bacon, lettuce, and tomatoe on one slice of toast and close the sando.
